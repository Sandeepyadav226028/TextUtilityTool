About TextUtility

TextUtility is your fast, free, and reliable online companion for editing and transforming text on the go. Whether you're a writer, student, developer, or content creator, this tool is built to streamline your workflow.

No Signups. No Ads. Just pure utility.

? Key Features:

  • Convert text to UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case, and Sentence case
  • Remove unwanted extra spaces
  • Live character and word count
  • Copy your result with a single click
  • Estimates reading time instantly
  • Responsive and mobile-friendly design

The tool is lightweight, runs entirely in your browser, and ensures your data never leaves your device. Perfect for quick editing tasks anytime, anywhere.
