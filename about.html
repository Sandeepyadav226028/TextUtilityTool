Get to Know Text Utility

Your free online text formatter & manipulator tool

What is Text Utility?

Text Utility is a simple, powerful online tool designed to make text formatting fast and effortless. It's not meant to replace full word processors, but it shines in speeding up repetitive text tasks like case changes, space trimming, and word/character counting.

Key Features

Word & Character Counter

Count words and characters instantly — great for essays, tweets, or character-limited messages.

Text Case Changer

Convert text to lowercase, UPPERCASE, Title Case, or Sentence Case in one click.

Remove Extra Spaces

Clean up unwanted spaces from your text or paragraphs quickly.

One-Click Copy

Copy the modified text instantly with a single button click.

Toggle Case

Flip all letters to their opposite case (UPPER to lower and vice versa).

Vowel & Consonant Count

Analyze your text for the number of vowels and consonants.

Palindrome Checker

Check if your input is a palindrome (reads the same backward).

Base64 Encode/Decode

Convert your text into Base64 format and decode it back safely.

Why Use Text Utility?

Whether you're a student, blogger, writer, or developer — Text Utility offers lightweight, effective formatting tools to make your life easier. And best of all? It’s completely free!
